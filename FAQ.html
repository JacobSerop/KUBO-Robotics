FAQ
Can KUBO be used to teach other subjects?

KUBO’s physical coding language, TagTiles, is flexible enough to teach a number of different subjects — from music to math, and everything in between! New subject and activity packages — including the soon-to-be-released language and spelling package — are always being developed by our expert team of educational consultants.

How does KUBO teach children to code?

KUBO and TagTiles teach children to code by simplifying complex concepts in coding and computational thinking. The tiles represent a few key concepts in coding — sequences, functions, loops, subroutines and recursive functions — and lay the foundations for computational literacy.

What is KUBO’s Coding License?

The Coding License is KUBO’s first subject package. It contains one introductory exercise and four sections that each highlight one of the four Cs of 21st-century education: communication, collaboration, creativity, and critical thinking. Once your students have successfully completed all sections, diplomas certifying their licence to code can be downloaded on KEDU!

How do I change my password?

To change your password, click on your name in the menu and press the menu item “change password”.
